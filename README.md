A chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate human conversation and help users by answering questions or performing simple tasks. 
It can communicate through text or voice and is commonly used in websites, apps, and customer support systems.
Basic chatbots follow predefined rules, while advanced chatbots use Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand user input more accurately and provide smarter responses. 
Chatbots are widely used for tasks like customer service, education, personal assistance, and entertainment, making interactions faster and more efficient for users.
